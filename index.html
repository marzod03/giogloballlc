About Us

Sergio Zamora
Founder and Lead Journalist

Gio Global LLC is a modern, digital-first news organization based in Los Angeles, California. We deliver relevant, timely and reliable information tailored for Mexican and Latino communities across the United States and Mexico. With no physical newsroom, we fully embrace decentralization and reach our audience directly through powerful digital platforms.

We are known for our clear, direct and courageous communication style. Our mission is to empower the Mexican diaspora with the information they need to stay informed, connected and represented in the sociopolitical landscape of both countries.

What We Do

Investigative Journalism
Deep research and fact-based stories uncovering the truths affecting our communities.
Political & Economic Analysis
We simplify the complex, from electoral systems to cross-border economics.
Video Reports
Dynamic and visual storytelling shared via YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram.
AI Integration
Using AI to streamline workflows and enhance data-driven reporting without sacrificing journalistic integrity.
