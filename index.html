About Us

Sergio Zamora
Founder and Lead Journalist

Gio Global LLC is a digital-first news organization based in Los Angeles, California, focused on delivering timely and relevant information to the Mexican and Latino communities in the U.S. and Mexico. With no physical newsroom, we embrace a decentralized, modern approach to journalism—one that thrives across digital platforms and speaks directly to the people.

We specialize in sociopolitical journalism, covering the major issues that affect daily life on both sides of the border. From U.S. immigration policies and elections to Mexico's public safety and economic realities, our reporting seeks to explain, contextualize, and amplify the stories that matter most to our audience.

Our content is designed to inform and empower. Whether you're watching a deep-dive report on YouTube, scrolling headlines on Facebook, or catching a quick update on TikTok, Gio Global LLC ensures you stay connected and informed. We are committed to independent journalism, free from corporate or political influence, and driven by the belief that truth and transparency build stronger communities.

What We Do

Investigative Journalism
We conduct in-depth investigations into complex topics affecting Mexican and Latino communities, exposing underlying issues in politics, security, immigration, and governance. Our journalism prioritizes truth, accountability, and the public interest.
Political and Economic Analysis
We break down political events, policy shifts, and economic trends in both Mexico and the United States. Our goal is to help our audience understand how these developments impact their daily lives, rights, and opportunities.
Video Reports
Through visually engaging and informative video content, we bring clarity and context to current events. These reports are crafted to be shared across platforms like YouTube, Facebook, and TikTok—making reliable information accessible to all.
AI-Generated Content
We leverage artificial intelligence to streamline the creation of news summaries, subtitles, data insights, and visual content. This allows us to expand our coverage while maintaining editorial standards and speed.
